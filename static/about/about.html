How to Use

Navbar

Click the logo / title to return to the home page

Learn how to solve the cube, view your profile, login or register from the homepage


Homepage

Press the spacebar / click the timer to start / stop it

The string at the top is the scramble string, put the white face at the top and green face on the front and apply the algorithm to scramble it

To help with this, an image of what the scramble looks like if done correctly is there (courtesy of VisualCube)


Sidebar

The sidebar is there to track your scores, scores can also be viewed in your profile page

Along with tracking your scores, it shows your mean time, and trimmed mean.

Trimmed mean is used in olympic events and speedcubing events alike. It is calculated as follows below (basically the mean without the best and worst)

Trimmed Mean = (Sum(Times) - Min(Times) - Max(Times)) / n


Security

Upon registering, your password will be stored as a salted hash

Nobody can access your profile or add / remove times from your history
